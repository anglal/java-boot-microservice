Create Topics & consumers on terminal
A. wrk-ms
1. Create Topic
/opt/homebrew/opt/kafka/bin/kafka-topics --create --topic kfk_topic --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --replication-factor 1 --partitions 1

2. Create Consumer
/opt/homebrew/opt/kafka/bin/kafka-console-consumer --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic kfk_topic --from-beginning

B. slry-ms

1. Create Topic
/opt/homebrew/opt/kafka/bin/kafka-topics --create --topic topic_slry --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --replication-factor 1 --partitions 1

2. Create Consumer
/opt/homebrew/opt/kafka/bin/kafka-console-consumer --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic topic_slry --from-beginning
